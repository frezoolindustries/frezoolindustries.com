Phenora

Premium home care solutions for the modern Indian home.

Clean Homes, Happy Lives

Phenora is Frezool Industries' flagship home care brand — conceived to deliver effective, safe, and pleasantly formulated cleaning products to Indian households. Every Phenora product is the result of careful formulation, rigorous testing, and a deep understanding of Indian cleaning needs.

The brand represents our commitment to bringing world-class hygiene solutions to everyday consumers at accessible price points, without ever compromising on quality or safety.

Brand Philosophy

Effectiveness first — products must deliver real cleaning power

Safety always — formulations tested for user and environmental safety

Accessible pricing — premium quality doesn't mean premium pricing

Indian-first design — made for Indian homes, Indian water, Indian conditions
