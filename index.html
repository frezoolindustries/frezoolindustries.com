Who We Are

Building India's Hygiene Infrastructure

Frezool Industries Private Limited is a professionally managed FMCG manufacturing enterprise headquartered in India. We specialize in the formulation, production, and distribution of hygiene and home care products that meet the highest standards of safety and efficacy.

With a focus on institutional trust, regulatory compliance, and scalable manufacturing, we serve B2B partners, distributors, and institutional buyers across the country.
